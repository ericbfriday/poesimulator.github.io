What is this?

Hi, I'm u/fish312. You may remember me for my Uber Elder Fight Simulator and my Tiny's Trial Crafting Simulator.

Well, I'm back again this league, with another poorly thought-out browser based atrocity. Inspired by the Twitch Plays Pokemon experiment of yesteryear, I present to you... PoESimulator - Reddit Plays PoE

Features

  • Free to play, straight from your web browser yet again
  • Join the queue, and take turns to control the exile
  • Click within the display window to move during your turn
  • Flasks (1-5) and skill keys (QWERT) supported, also (I) for inventory and (P) for passive tree
  • Some parts of the screen are unclickable to prevent potential griefing
  • Running on a potato PC, because EC2 instances with graphics cards are expensive
  • SSF for the true exile experience
  • Mobile devices not officially supported

How far will the collective minds of Reddit progress? How many exiles does it take to save Wraeclast? Let's find out.
